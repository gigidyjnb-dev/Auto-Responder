GETTING STARTED

How to Set Up Auto-Responder

Follow these steps to sync your marketplace listings and start getting automated replies.

1

Sign Up & Subscribe

Create your account and choose a subscription plan that fits your needs.

Why subscribe?

Free accounts are limited to 5 listings. Pro accounts get unlimited listings, priority support, and advanced features.

Create Account View Plans
2

Install Browser Extension

Download and install our Chrome extension to sync your marketplace listings automatically.

Chrome Required

Currently only available for Google Chrome. Firefox support coming soon.

Download Extension Chrome Web Store
3

Configure Extension

Open the extension popup and enter your account details.

  1. Click the extension icon in your Chrome toolbar
  2. Enter your email and password
  3. Enter your app URL (provided after signup)
  4. Click "One-Click Setup"

Your App URL

After signing up, you'll receive your unique app URL. It looks like: https://your-app-name.railway.app

4

Sync Your Listings

Go to your Facebook Marketplace listings page and let the extension sync everything.

  1. Open Facebook Marketplace → Your Listings
  2. Scroll down to load ALL your listings
  3. Click the extension icon
  4. Click "One-Click Setup" or "Re-Sync Listings"

What happens next?

The extension automatically extracts all your listings, uploads them to your account, and turns on auto-reply mode.

5

Customize Responses (Optional)

Fine-tune how the AI responds to different types of buyer questions.

The AI is pre-trained to handle common marketplace scenarios:

  • Price negotiations
  • Availability questions
  • Shipping inquiries
  • Condition descriptions
  • Pickup arrangements
Customize Settings
6

Monitor & Manage

Keep an eye on your automated responses and manage your listings.

Dashboard Features

  • View all synced listings
  • Monitor response statistics
  • Review pending replies (if auto-send is off)
  • Update listing details
  • Manage subscription
Open Dashboard View Listings
7

Go Live!

Keep Facebook Marketplace inbox open and let the automation handle buyer messages.

How it works

The extension monitors your Facebook inbox for new messages. When a buyer asks about one of your synced listings, the AI generates a personalized response using your listing details.

Response Examples

Buyer: "Is this still available?" AI: "Yes, this Sony WH-1000XM5 Headphones is still available! It's in excellent condition with original packaging. The listed price is $180. Would you like to arrange pickup?" Buyer: "Can you do $150?" AI: "The headphones are priced at $180 for the excellent condition. I'm open to reasonable offers - what's your best offer?"
