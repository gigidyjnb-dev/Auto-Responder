FAQ

Before you launch

Do I need OpenAI? No. The app can reply with built-in fallback logic, and OpenAI improves response quality when configured.
How do subscriptions work? Paid checkout is hosted through Stripe. If Stripe is not configured yet, the pricing page will tell you before attempting a live checkout.
Can I test before paying? Yes. The free plan is intended for exactly that, with a 5-listing cap.
